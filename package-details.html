Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Departure from Kashmir

Depart from Srinagar International Airport to Jeddah. Upon arrival, transfer to Madinah. Check into hotel and rest.

Day 2-4: Spiritual Days in Madinah

Visit Masjid an-Nabawi, participate in prayers, and visit historical sites including Quba Mosque and Uhud Mountain.

Day 5: Travel to Makkah

Travel to Makkah by luxury coach. Perform Umrah upon arrival with guidance from our scholars.

Day 6-8: Hajj Rituals Begin

Enter state of Ihram, travel to Mina, then to Arafat for Wuquf. Stay overnight at Muzdalifah and proceed to Jamarat for Rami.

Day 9-11: Tashreeq Days

Perform Tawaf al-Ifadah and Sa'ee. Stay in Mina for three days of Tashreeq, performing Rami each day.

Day 12-14: Farewell Tawaf & Return

Perform farewell Tawaf. Free time for shopping and final prayers. Depart from Jeddah to Srinagar.
