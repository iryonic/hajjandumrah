About Noorul Haram

Discover our story, mission, and the dedicated team behind your spiritual journeys

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Kashmir, Noorul Haram began with a simple yet profound vision: to help fellow Muslims fulfill their spiritual obligations of Hajj and Umrah with ease, comfort, and devotion.

What started as a small travel agency has grown into a trusted name in pilgrimage travel, serving thousands of pilgrims from across Jammu & Kashmir and beyond.

Our journey has been guided by the principles of integrity, compassion, and excellence in service. We understand that a pilgrimage is not just a journey, but a once-in-a-lifetime spiritual experience that requires meticulous planning and heartfelt care.

"Our mission is to make your spiritual journey as smooth and meaningful as possible, so you can focus entirely on your devotion and connection with Allah."

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To provide exceptional Hajj and Umrah services that combine comfort, spiritual guidance, and authentic cultural experiences, making each pilgrimage a truly transformative journey.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and respected Hajj and Umrah service provider, recognized for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and the spiritual well-being of every pilgrim.

Our Values

Integrity, Compassion, Excellence, Spirituality, and Community. These values guide every decision we make and every service we provide to our pilgrims.
